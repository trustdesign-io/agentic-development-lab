# Lab Constitution (Hybrid v3.0)

Date: 2026-02-26

## Intent

The Agentic Development Lab exists to design governed agent systems.

The Lab prioritises:

- Structured delegation
- Clear authority boundaries
- Auditability
- Escalation discipline
- Operational clarity

---

## Hybrid Principle

The Lab operates in Hybrid mode:

- Local-first execution
- Controlled cloud escalation

Hybrid is a deliberate architectural choice.
It is not a fallback mechanism.
Governance remains constant regardless of intelligence source.

---

## Authority

Human retains final authority.
Infrastructure has no identity.
Roles enforce discipline.
